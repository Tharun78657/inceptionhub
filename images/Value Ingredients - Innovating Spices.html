About Us

Welcome to Value Ingredients

Value Ingredients, along with its subsidiaries Nature Log Organics and Value Premier Labs, is a leading player in the spice ingredients industry. Established in 2014 with a mission to improve consumer health and uplift marginal farmers, we offer a fully digitised and transparent supply chain for high-quality spices. Today, we are among the largest processors of pesticide residue-free and certified organic spice ingredients from India.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred provider or Sustainable,Flavourful, and healthy Ingredients, delivering Integrity, Quality and Value to brands and consumers
